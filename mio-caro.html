mio caro

about

project

It is an instructional design project for a fictional online women's clothing store of the brand called MIO CARO. The challenge was to combine a simple and user-friendly interface with a modern design.

As a result, the main screen was created in three versions and a catalog with a full range of clothes, a product card, a basket, an order page and others.

In work used photographs from this website www.mesdemoisellesparis.com and some from the photographer by Olga Dieterle.

first screen

fonts and colors

This choice of colors can be explained by the fact that shades of purple and pink evokes a sense of elegance and top class. This is a way to attract wealthy customers to a one-page selling website.

Intense blue is refreshing and energizing, navy blue is great for corporate websites or other occasions where strength and reliability are needed in designs.
