project

It is a simple landing page design designed for an online potted cactus delivery service. The presentation presents several main screens, service benefits, product cards and other website design pages.

first screen

fonts and colors

This choice of colors can be explained by the fact that shades of purple and pink evokes a sense of elegance and top class. This is a way to attract wealthy customers to a one-page selling website.

Brighter shades of green are more energetic, while olive shades are usually associated with the natural world. Dark green looks more stable and presentable.
